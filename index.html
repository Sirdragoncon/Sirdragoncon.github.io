All about Cheetahs

Cheetahs are really fast, they can run at speeds up to
65MPH. To hunt their prey they stalk it first then run until either the prey or the Cheetah give up.

Cheetah cubs can sometimes be attacked or eaten by hyenas which happen to be one of their biggest predators!

Cheetahs are spotted to camoflauge themselves and hide from other predators.

Cheetahs can accelerate from 0 to 70mph in less than 3 seconds.

Cheetahs typically weigh between 84 to 134 pounds.
The male Cheetahs are larger than the females.
